# Reference:
Hsieh, C. Y., Marelli, M., & Rastle, K. (2025). Compositional processing in the recognition of Chinese compounds: Behavioural and computational studies. Psychonomic Bulletin & Review, 32(4), 1878-1889. https://doi.org/10.3758/s13423-025-02668-8

# Description
- This paper contains meaningfulness ratings for Chinese two-character novel compound words.
- There are three subsets of 500 novel compounds. Each subset was responded by 10 participants. A total of 30 participants were recruited in this rating task.
