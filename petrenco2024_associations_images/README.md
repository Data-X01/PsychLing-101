# Reference:
Petrenco, A., Fischer, A. & Guenther, F. (2024, October 24). Effects of Lexicalized Labels on Novel Concept Representation. Retrieved from osf.io/tv4kc



# Description
- In a continued free association task, participants are presented with AI-generated images of novel concepts, specifically, novel creatures or situations, such as a sharp drill attempting either to create or destroy another world inside a mirror. In the positive and negative label conditions, a label carrying positive or negative valence, respectively, is presented under each image 3 seconds after the image presentation. In the no-label condition, the image is presented without a label. Participants are asked to generate 3 associations to the cue concepts.
- The images were used as cues in response to which participants produced associations. The size of the image was reduced to ≤ 100 KB each for the purposes of PsychLing101.
- 19440 data points from 270 participants
